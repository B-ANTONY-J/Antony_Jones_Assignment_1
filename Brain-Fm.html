Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments

We look at the effects of out technology on the brain, using
fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests,
measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments always include a control condition
('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
